The JELD-WEN 32 in. x 80 in. White Pre-Hung Right-Hand Inswing Premium Fan Lite Entry Door with Brickmold features a deco-inspired glass fan insert above an elegant 4-panel design. The 1-piece polystyrene core provides long-lasting insulation, and the galvanized steel surface resists rust and corrosion. Weather stripping keeps your home warm in the winter and cool in the summer. Installation is convenient thanks to the pre-hung design.

  • 32 in. x 80 in.
  • 1-Piece polystyrene core provides long-lasting insulation. Galvanized steel resists rust and corrosion
  • Adjustable sills protect against the elements
  • Weather stripping keeps your home warm in the winter and cool in the summer
  • Pre-hung and ready to install with a white wood frame
  • 4-panel design creates classic style
  • Dual-pane insulated glass insert
  • Aluminum-finished sills and brass-finished hinges
  • 2 coats of baked-on enamel primer allow even painting or staining
  • Requires a 34-1/2 in. x 82-1/2 in. rough opening for a secure fit
  • Brickmold enhances insulation
  • Energy Star compliant, making this item available for a tax credit
